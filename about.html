Linkr URL Manager

About

Linkr URL Manager is a lightweight browser extension that helps you organize and access your favorite websites with just one click. It offers a more visual and customizable alternative to traditional bookmarks.

How to Use

Add URLs

Click the purple plus button to expand the form, enter a name and URL, then click "Add URL".

Open Websites

Click on any button to open the associated website in a new tab.

Reorder URLs

Click and drag any URL to change its position in the list.

Edit & Delete

Use the pencil icon to edit or the trash icon to delete a URL.

Tips & Tricks

  • Press Enter while in the name or URL field to quickly add a new URL
  • Use complete URLs including the protocol (e.g., https://)
  • Organize related links by dragging them next to each other
  • The extension syncs across your devices when signed into Chrome
